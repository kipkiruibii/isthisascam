Website Scanner

Overview:

This feature provides a detailed analysis of any website to help you identify potential scams and fraud. By evaluating various aspects of the site, we help you make informed decisions before interacting with it. Whether you're considering making a purchase, entering personal information, or just browsing, our scan will ensure you're protected.

What to Expect:

1. Domain Information:

  • Domain Age & Registrar: Find out when the domain was registered and who owns it. Recently registered domains or those with hidden ownership details can be red flags.

  • Blacklist Status: Check if the domain is known for malicious activity or reported as a scam .

2. SSL Certificate Validation:

  • SSL Details: Verify if the site uses a secure HTTPS connection with a valid SSL certificate. This ensures that data transmitted between your browser and the site is encrypted.

  • Certificate Validity: See the certificate issuer and its validity period to ensure the site is using up-to-date security measures.

3. Content Analysis:

  • Scam Indicators: Analyze the site's content for common scam patterns such as urgent calls to action, unrealistic promises, or requests for personal information.

  • Language & Quality: Detect suspicious language and evaluate the overall quality of the site's content for signs of fraud.

4. Redirection and Links:

  • Redirection Patterns: Check if the site redirects you to other pages or sites, especially those that seem suspicious or unrelated.

  • External Links: Identify links to other domains and evaluate their safety. This helps to uncover potential phishing attempts or malware distribution.

5. Third-Party Resources:

  • External Scripts: Review third-party scripts and resources used by the site to ensure they are from reputable sources. Malicious scripts can compromise your security.

How It Helps:

  • Avoid Fraud: Protect yourself from fraudulent websites by identifying suspicious domains and content before you interact with them.

  • Secure Transactions: Ensure that any transactions or data submissions are done through secure, legitimate sites.

  • Informed Decisions: Make better decisions about which sites to trust and which to avoid based on detailed, actionable insights.

Getting Started:

Simply enter the URL of the website you want to scan into our Website Scan feature. Our system will analyze the site and provide you with a detailed report on its safety and legitimacy. Stay safe!
